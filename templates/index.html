Overview

The Dog Movement Dashboard visualizes adoption and transport trends across states and shelters in the United States. It provides insights to help state representatives, policymakers, and animal welfare organizations make data-driven decisions to improve adoption rates and reduce overcrowding in shelters.

State Representatives can analyze state-level data to understand how dogs move across state lines, identify adoption patterns, and highlight opportunities for targeted support or regional collaboration. Animal Shelters can explore shelter-level data to assess their intake and adoption flows, transfer activity, and key adoption drivers.

Data Source

The dataset powering this dashboard comes from the Dog Adoption Dataset on Kaggle . It includes detailed information on breed, size, age, and adoption status across U.S. shelters. The data has been cleaned, standardized, and aggregated to support both state-level and shelter-level analyses.
